English II

Light

Alone in the dark woods,
I long for a way out
Stepping here, looking there,
I see nothing but black

But wait,
Over there, a light flicker
Beckons at me

That light,
It feels warm and familiar
Cozy fires,
And soothing voices
Float in my head

I step towards the light,
It grows larger and larger,
Brighter and brighter,

Until it's not black anymore,
But rather white
And there's a woman,
With her arms spread out,
And it's you, Mum

It was you.
